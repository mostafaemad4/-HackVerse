Introduction to Hacking

Hacking is an exciting world full of challenges and technical creativity. This field refers to the ability to explore and analyze technological systems with the aim of improving their security or exploiting their vulnerabilities. Despite the stereotype that associates hacking with illegal activities, hacking is not necessarily a negative thing; it includes different types, some of which aim to protect data and systems. On this site, we will take you on a journey to explore the world of hacking, from its definition and types to its tools and techniques. You will learn the difference between ethical hacking that protects systems and black hacking that penetrates them for malicious purposes, in addition to other types such as gray and political hacking. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive understanding of this controversial field and how to use it safely and ethically. Whether you are a beginner looking for knowledge, or a professional wanting to deepen your skills, this site is your first destination for the world of hacking.

Road Map

Networking

Start by studying networking basics such as protocols (TCP/IP, HTTP, DNS). Understand how firewalls and intrusion detection systems (IDS) work

Learn Operating Systems

Focus on Linux because it is the platform for many hacking tools. Start with distributions like Kali Linux and Ubuntu. Learn basic Bash commands and how to navigate the system..

Learn programming

Programming proficiency is essential to understand how applications work and to write hacking tools. To create scripts and tools and to understand website-related attacks. To deal with low-level vulnerabilities

Types of hacker

Black Hacker

It is a term used to describe individuals who hack into systems and networks for illegal or malicious purposes.

White Hacker

He is a person who specializes in testing the security of systems and networks using his hacking skills, but in a legal and ethical manner..

Green Hacker

A is a term used to refer to beginners in the field of hacking, who do not have extensive experience or advanced skills in hacking techniques

Red Hacker

It is a type that refers to hackers who combine the skills of penetration, attack, and defense, but who focus specifically on protecting systems and confronting other hackers..

Blue hacker

It is a term used to refer to individuals or teams who specialize in cyber defense and protecting systems and networks from attacks. They are also known as cyber defenders

Elite Hacker

It is a term given to highly skilled and experienced hackers in the world of cybersecurity. These individuals represent the pinnacle of excellence in the field.
